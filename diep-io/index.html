WHAT IS DIEP IO?

Diep.io unblocked is a multiplayer web-based tank-battle game. Players control a tank and use it to shoot down enemies and obstacles. The game features a variety of different weapons and maps, as well as leaderboards and achievements. The goal of the game is to survive as long as possible and get the highest score. 1. Go to the diep.io website and click "Play." This will open the game in a new window. 2. Choose your tank class. There are four classes to choose from, each with its own unique characteristics. 3. Use the arrow keys to move your tank around the map. 4. Use the left mouse button to shoot bullets at other tanks. 5. Collect XP by destroying other tanks and objects on the map. This will allow you to upgrade your tank. 6. Upgrade your tank by pressing the upgrade button in the top-right corner of the screen. 7. Survive as long as possible and earn a higher score by destroying other tanks and collecting XP.
